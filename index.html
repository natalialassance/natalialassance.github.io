I enjoy the process

Creativity to find solutions coherent to users' expectations
  • 1. Immersion
    Understanding the problem, the context, the client expectations and his target by research, interviews, tests and analysis.
  • 2. Explosion
    Black Box Thinking: the knowledge gotten at the first phase is controlled input for the uncontrolled and creative outputs.
  • 3. Decision
    White Box Thinking: phase of selecting greatest ideas, testing them with rapid prototyping, choosing the best potential solution for development.
  • 4. Finishing
    Testing the best solution with users to identify the result with their need and with their expectations.
